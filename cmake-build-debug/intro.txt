Hello Adventurer!

Welcome to my game.

Your job is collect number of space junks and go back home before your fuel  finish.
Be careful! few big asteroid are flying too. If they touch you , game over.

--- Instructions How to play ---

- Use W,A,S,D for movements :
  W = up,
  A = left,
  S = down,
  D = right
- Every move consumes fuel depends on chosen difficulty.
- If fuel is zero, you lose
- Pick up junk to get more fuel depends on difficulty
- Asteroid move same time with you, one cell every your move
